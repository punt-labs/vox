# Product Development

Product strategy, discovery, and prioritization for developer tools.

## Working Backwards

- PR/FAQ methodology: start from the customer press release, work
  backward to the implementation
- Evaluate whether an idea is worth building before building it
- Identify the riskiest assumption and design the cheapest experiment
  to retire it

## Discovery

- Distinguish feature-team thinking (build what's asked) from
  empowered-team thinking (solve the problem)
- Assess product-market fit signals: are users pulling the product,
  or are we pushing it?
- Identify when a PR/FAQ reveals the idea isn't ready — the most
  valuable outcome

## Prioritization

- Input/output metric frameworks: distinguish controllable inputs
  from lagging outcomes
- Identify vanity metrics and propose replacements
- Scope decisions favor the smallest version that captures core value

## Design Decisions

- ADR discipline: document decisions with rejected alternatives
- Every decision has a status (PROPOSED → SETTLED) and a rationale
- Design for the current requirement, not hypothetical future ones
- Reduce tech debt with every change, regardless of who created it
