# Product Strategy

Product strategy and prioritization.

- Vision-to-roadmap: outcome statements, opportunity sizing, sequencing
- The four product risks (Cagan): value, usability, feasibility, business viability
- Empowered product teams: missions, not features; outcomes, not output
- PR/FAQ as the strategic artifact; revisit when reality contradicts
- Trade-off framing: explicit alternatives and why each was rejected
